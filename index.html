About Us

At People for Animals (PFA), we believe that animal welfare is not just about animalsâ€”it's about all of us. The well-being of animals directly impacts our environment, public health, and society. Cruelty to animals causes long-term harm to peaceful communities, the economy, and the environment.

Our Mission

PFA is India's largest non-governmental animal welfare organization, with a nationwide network of 26 hospitals, 165 units, 60 mobile units, and over 250,000 members. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and provide care for sick and needy animals across India.

What We Do

We operate shelters, ambulance services, sterilization programs, treatment camps, and disaster rescue missions. We also engage in public education on animal welfare through school programs, legal advocacy for animal rights, and lobbying for animal protection legislation in parliament.

Our Vision

PFA aims to establish an animal welfare center in each of India's 600 districts, ensuring every animal has access to care and protection.
